Pasta

Pasta recipe

Pasta, a beloved staple in many cuisines worldwide, offers endless possibilities for delicious meals. One particularly enticing dish is pasta with creamy tomato sauce—a delightful combination of flavors and textures that never fails to satisfy the taste buds. Let's delve into the culinary journey of preparing this mouthwatering dish. To embark on this culinary adventure, one must first gather the necessary ingredients. The list is simple yet essential: pasta of your choice, olive oil, garlic, onion, canned diced tomatoes, heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, fresh basil, salt, and black pepper. With these ingredients at hand, the stage is set for a culinary masterpiece.

Ingredients:

  • 250g pasta (any shape)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the pasta to the boiling water and cook according to the package instructions until al dente.
  3. While the pasta is cooking, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add minced garlic and chopped onion to the skillet. Sauté until the onion is translucent and the garlic is fragrant.
  5. Add diced tomatoes to the skillet and cook until they start to soften.
  6. Reduce the heat to low and pour in the heavy cream. Stir to combine.
  7. Add grated Parmesan cheese to the skillet and stir until melted and the sauce is smooth.
  8. Season the sauce with salt and black pepper to taste.
  9. Once the pasta is cooked, drain it and add it to the skillet with the sauce.
  10. Stir the pasta into the sauce until well coated and heated through.
  11. Remove from heat and sprinkle chopped fresh basil over the pasta.
  12. Serve hot, garnished with additional Parmesan cheese and basil if desired.
